I am NIKKIE and this is my story.

Hi! I am Mary Nicole Yabut, a web developer, and an aspiring photographer based in Rizal, Philippines.

As a web developer, it has been my dream since I was in junior high school. Editing HTML + CSS properties while using Tumblr has been my pastime and learning deeper about these codes made HTML + CSS closer to my heart. Now that I learn even more than Tumblr codes, I saw how flexible HTML + CSS are, and how they are easily manipulated. I used to develop Wordpress websites for my previous websites which are taken down already, Carrd website for fun.

As a photographer, I use nature and landscape as my main subjects. Seeing the colors blue and green calms me down. The unique characteristics of nature gives us the message of the possibility of impossible. As such, taking photos of beautiful things related to nature has been one of my hobbies. I also do landscape photography wherein I include the sky.
